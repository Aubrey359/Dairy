Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can place orders through our website, by calling us, or by visiting our farm. We also offer delivery services for your convenience.

Do you deliver?

Yes! We offer local delivery services within a 50-mile radius. Delivery fees vary by distance and order size.

Are your products organic?

We practice sustainable farming methods and are working towards full organic certification. All our products are grown with minimal chemical inputs.

Can I visit the farm?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors and offer farm tours. Please contact us to schedule your visit.
