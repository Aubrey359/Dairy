Frequently Asked Questions

Products

What types of coffee seedlings do you offer?

We offer high-quality Arabica and Robusta coffee seedlings. Our seedlings are disease-resistant and well-adapted to local growing conditions. We also provide mixed variety packs for farmers who want to diversify their coffee production.

Are your products organic?

We practice sustainable farming methods and are working towards full organic certification. All our products are grown with minimal chemical inputs, focusing on natural pest control and soil health. We use compost and organic fertilizers whenever possible.

What fresh produce do you have available?

Our fresh produce varies by season and includes avocados, pineapples, various vegetables, and seasonal fruits. We also offer hay and silage for livestock. Check our products page or contact us for current availability.

Do you offer livestock products?

Yes! We offer fresh cow milk, chicken eggs, fresh chicken, and fish. All our livestock products come from animals raised in healthy, natural conditions with proper care and feeding.

Ordering & Delivery

How can I place an order?

You can place orders through our website using the order form, by calling us directly, or by visiting our farm. We also accept orders through email and social media. Visit our Order/Booking page to get started.

Do you deliver products?

Yes, we deliver coffee seedlings, fresh produce, and other products within a 50-mile radius of our farm. Delivery fees vary by distance and order size. We also offer express delivery for urgent orders.

What is your minimum order quantity?

For coffee seedlings, we have a minimum order of 100 seedlings. For fresh produce, there's no minimum order, but we offer better prices for larger quantities. Contact us for specific minimums on other products.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available within 1-2 days for an additional fee. Same-day delivery may be available for local orders placed before 2 PM.

Can I track my order?

Yes! You can track your order using the order number we provide. Use the order tracking feature on our Order page or contact us directly for updates.

Farm Visits

Can I visit the farm?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors and offer various types of farm experiences including tours, educational visits, farm stays, and group visits. Contact us to schedule your visit or use our booking form.

What types of farm visits do you offer?

We offer farm tours (1-2 hours), educational visits (3-4 hours), farm stays (overnight), group visits (5+ people), and private tours. Each experience is tailored to your interests and group size.

How much do farm visits cost?

Farm visits range from $25-50 per person depending on the type and duration. Group discounts are available for 10+ people. Farm stays include accommodation and meals at additional cost.

Do you offer farm stays and accommodation?

Yes! We have comfortable accommodation available for farm stays. Our rental houses and BnB options provide a unique farm experience with modern amenities. Book through our booking system.

Farming & Agriculture

What farming methods do you use?

We practice sustainable agriculture focusing on soil health, water conservation, and biodiversity. Our methods include crop rotation, composting, natural pest control, and minimal chemical inputs. We're committed to environmentally responsible farming.

Do you provide farming consultation services?

Yes! We offer consultation services for sustainable farming practices, crop selection, soil management, and farm planning. We also provide training workshops for local farmers and agricultural students.

How do you ensure product quality?

We maintain strict quality standards from seed selection to harvest. Our products are regularly tested, properly stored, and handled with care. We use sustainable practices that enhance both quality and environmental health.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), bank transfers, and credit/debit cards. Payment is typically due upon delivery or pickup, though we may require deposits for large orders.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer discounts for bulk orders. Coffee seedlings have better prices for orders of 500+ seedlings. Fresh produce discounts start at 20+ boxes. Contact us for specific bulk pricing.

Are your prices negotiable?

Our prices are competitive and fair. For large orders or long-term partnerships, we may offer special pricing. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and we'll work with you to find a suitable arrangement.

General

What are your business hours?

We're open Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM, and closed on Sundays. Farm visits can be scheduled outside regular hours by appointment.

How can I get updates about new products?

Subscribe to our newsletter for regular updates about new products, seasonal availability, and farm news. You can also follow us on social media or check our website regularly.

Do you offer educational programs?

Yes! We offer educational programs for schools, universities, and community groups. Our programs cover sustainable agriculture, farm-to-table concepts, and environmental stewardship. Contact us to arrange educational visits.

How can I become a partner or supplier?

We're always interested in partnerships with local farmers, suppliers, and businesses. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities, including supply agreements, joint ventures, or collaborative projects.
